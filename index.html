Lusell Mata

REALTY ASSOCIATES

Lusell Mata is described by friends, family and clients as an individual that has great ethical guidelines, is hardworking, and has strong negotiating service skills in every detail of the real estate process. Lusell has been a part of the Houston community for over 20 years and is passionate about serving this beautiful city and surrounding areas. Her career began in the Real Estate Title Industry where she developed a passion for helping buyers and sellers with their transactions. Her experience in Real Estate sales has increased rapidly in the past 5 years earning several realtor designations including Rookie of the year, Top Producer in 2016, TRLP Texas Real Estate Leadership program an intensive 10 month curriculum of leadership and service which created a legacy project for the Homeless in Houston via www.DHE2017.com for New Hope Housing raising over 50K for the 2017 year, ABR Accredited Buyer Representative and GRI Graduate Realtor Institute program all which have resulted in great reviews from her past clients valuing her knowledge, communication and negotiation skills in the residential sales market. Lusell is a family oriented woman with a devotion to God, Family and community. Her focus and priorities are to constantly improve through continuing education in all areas of personal and business everyday life, her passion to help others continues to be strengthened through service and her love to see people succeed in their real estate goals.

Graduate Realtor Institute

Members involved in residental real estate who want a solid base of information for their practice will want to participate in the REALTOR Institute Program and earn the GRI designation NATIONAL ASSOCIATION OF REALTORS. Contact your State REALTOR Association for course dates and locations or go to the listing of State REALTOR Associations available here. 

Texas Realtors Leadership Program

The mission of the Texas REALTORS Leadership Program is to develop community and organizational leaders to become contributors to their community and their businesses and enrich their personal and professional lives. Participants will learn about their communication skills, how to lead a team and work within a team, understand multi-cultures, and how to grow their real estate business.
